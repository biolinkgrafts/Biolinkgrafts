About Biolink

Biolink is an independent allograft tissue distributor serving surgery centers and hospitals across the Midwest. We specialize in providing premium orthobiologic products for spine, orthopedic, and sports medicine procedures.

All of our tissue is sourced from Alamo Biologics and processed by Precision Allograft Solutions, maintaining the highest standards of safety and quality. We offer competitive pricing, reliable delivery, and the personalized service that only an independent distributor can provide.

Whether you're a small ASC or a regional hospital system, we're committed to being a responsive, reliable partner for your allograft needs.

Product Catalog

We distribute a comprehensive range of allograft tissue products across multiple categories. All products are FDA-compliant and sourced from AATB-accredited tissue banks. Contact us for detailed pricing, availability, and technical specifications.

DBM Putty

Demineralized bone matrix putty in multiple sizes (0.5cc – 10cc). Available in standard and Crush-Mix formulations for bone void filling and fusion applications.

Bio-Matrix Fibers

Demineralized cortical fiber products for bone void filling. Available in 2cc, 5cc, 10cc, and 20cc configurations with excellent handling characteristics.

Cancellous Chips & Cubes

Cancellous bone chips and cubes in multiple grit sizes and volumes. Ideal for bone grafting applications requiring structural support and osteoconductivity.

Structural Cortical Grafts

Cloward dowels, tricortical blocks, cortical struts, and structural long bone grafts for spine and orthopedic reconstruction procedures.

Sports Medicine Grafts

Tendon allografts including Achilles, patella, semitendinosus, gracilis, and ACL reconstruction grafts. Available in PASCO® and irradiated processing.

Soft Tissue & Amnion

Fascia lata, pericardium, and amniotic membrane grafts for soft tissue reconstruction, wound care, and regenerative medicine applications.
